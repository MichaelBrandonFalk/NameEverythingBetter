Naming Conventions

This page explains the browser logic behind both sides of the public tool: Neb for movies and captions, and Verso for art. It is meant to be readable on the web without needing the downloadable Mac apps.

Shared Cleanup Rules

Formatting

  • Lowercase everything.
  • Spaces become underscores.
  • Repeated underscores are collapsed to a single underscore.
  • Accents are stripped to their base letters.
  • Apostrophes are removed instead of becoming separators.

Symbol Handling

  • & becomes and.
  • @ becomes at.
  • Standalone + becomes and.
  • Attached + becomes plus.
  • Middle ... becomes an underscore only when it sits between letters or numbers.
  • Trailing ..., and regular . or .., are removed instead of becoming separators.
  • !, ., ·, and * are removed.

Neb: Movies and Captions

For episodic naming, the title field should contain the series title. That applies to episode video names and episode caption names.

In the single-item browser tool, supported video tasks show the MOV output first, then the English caption name, then the Spanish caption name.

Language now applies across the full Neb MOV and caption set. MOV files end with _eng or _las, and Spanish caption filenames also carry _las in the main stem before resolution.

Type Formula
Movie[title]_feature_[resolution]_[house_number]_[eng|las].mov
Caption[title]_feature[_las]_[resolution]_[house_number]_[cc|sub]_[eng|las].vtt
Dub Audio[title]_feature_[resolution]_[house_number]_dub_las.wav
Episode[series_title]_s[xx]_e[xx]_[resolution]_[house_number]_[eng|las].mov
Episode Caption[series_title]_s[xx]_e[xx][_las]_[resolution]_[house_number]_[cc|sub]_[eng|las].vtt
Original Premium Series (Yearly)[title]_s[yyyy]_e[xx]_[resolution]_[house_number]_[eng|las].mov
Exclusive Conversation (Yearly)exclusive_conversations_s[yyyy]_e[xx]_[interviewees]_[resolution]_[house_number]_[eng|las].mov
Virtual Screening[title]_virtual_screening_[resolution]_[house_number]_[eng|las].mov
Virtual Screening Episode[series_title]_s[xx]_e[xx]_virtual_screening_[resolution]_[house_number]_[eng|las].mov
Virtual Screening Episode Caption[series_title]_s[xx]_e[xx]_virtual_screening[_las]_[resolution]_[house_number]_[cc|sub]_[eng|las].vtt

Verso: Art

For season and episode art, the title field should contain the series title. Language is always included before the art tag as _eng or _las.

Type Formula
Movie[title]_[eng|las]_[art_tag]_[aspect_ratio]_[dimensions].[ext]
Series[title]_[eng|las]_[art_tag]_[aspect_ratio]_[dimensions].[ext]
Season Placeholder[series_title]_s[xx]_[eng|las]_[art_tag]_[aspect_ratio]_[dimensions].[ext]
Episode[series_title]_s[xx]_e[xx]_[eng|las]_[art_tag]_[aspect_ratio]_[dimensions].[ext]
Original Premium Series (Yearly)[title]_s[yyyy]_e[xx]_[eng|las]_[art_tag]_[aspect_ratio]_[dimensions].[ext]

Virtual Screening[title]_virtual_screening_[eng|las]_[art_tag]_[aspect_ratio]_[dimensions].[ext]
Virtual Screening Episode[series_title]_s[xx]_e[xx]_virtual_screening_[eng|las]_[art_tag]_[aspect_ratio]_[dimensions].[ext]
Trailer[title]_trailer_[eng|las]_[art_tag]_[aspect_ratio]_[dimensions].[ext]
Extras[title]_[extra_prefix]_[eng|las]_[art_tag]_[aspect_ratio]_[dimensions].[ext]
Carousel[title]_carousel_[eng|las]_[art_tag]_[aspect_ratio]_[dimensions].[ext]

Art Tags

  • ca = Cover Art
  • bg = Background Art
  • tt = Title Treatment

Extensions

  • tt always outputs .png.
  • ca and bg output .jpg.
